Charles Walkden's webpage
I work in ergodic theory and dynamical systems. I am interested in skew-product dynamical systems, in particular in their ergodic-theoretic and probabilistic properties, and applications to iterated function systems.
On September 10th-11th 2008, I am organising a MIMS-funded workshop on ergodic theory and probabilistic aspects of skew-product dynamics. Click here for details.
